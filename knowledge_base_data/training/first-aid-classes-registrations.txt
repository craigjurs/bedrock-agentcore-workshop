HOW TO REGISTER FOR TRAINING CLASSES

Online Registration:
1. Visit redcross.org/take-a-class
2. Search for course by name, location, or date
3. Select desired class from available options
4. Create account or log in to existing account
5. Complete registration form
6. Make payment online (credit card, debit card, PayPal)
7. Receive confirmation email with class details and student account information
8. Access student portal for class materials and updates

Phone Registration:
Call 1-800-RED-CROSS (1-800-733-2767)
Available Monday-Friday 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM EST
Have course name, preferred date, and payment method ready
Receive confirmation via email or phone

In-Person Registration:
Visit any Red Cross Training Center during business hours
Speak with training coordinator
Complete registration form
Make payment (cash, check, credit card)
Receive confirmation receipt and student account information

CANCELLATION AND RESCHEDULING

Cancellation Policy:
- Cancel at least 48 hours before class start time for full refund
- Cancellations within 48 hours: 50% refund or reschedule option
- No-show: No refund, reschedule available for additional fee
- Emergency cancellations: Contact training center for special consideration

Rescheduling:
- Available up to 24 hours before class start time
- Subject to availability in other classes
- Rescheduling fee: $25.00 (waived for emergency situations)
- Reschedule online through student account or by phone

COURSE DESCRIPTIONS

Adult and Pediatric First Aid/CPR/AED:
Comprehensive course covering first aid, CPR, and AED use for adults, children, and infants. Includes hands-on practice, written exam, and skills evaluation. Certification valid for 2 years.

Basic Life Support (BLS) for Healthcare Providers:
Advanced course for healthcare professionals. Covers high-quality CPR, team dynamics, and advanced life support techniques. Includes written and skills testing.

First Aid/CPR/AED - Blended Learning:
Combines online learning with in-person skills session. Complete online portion at your own pace, then attend skills session for hands-on practice and certification.

Wilderness and Remote First Aid:
Advanced course for outdoor enthusiasts and professionals. Covers extended care, environmental emergencies, and improvised techniques. Two-day intensive course.

Pediatric First Aid/CPR/AED:
Specialized course focusing on first aid and CPR for infants and children. Ideal for childcare providers, parents, and teachers. Includes child-specific emergency scenarios.
